Introducing Maestro: A better mail experience for Windows Phone 8.

View Demo

There has to be a better way.

Managing emails on your Windows Phone can be a pain. That’s why we’re creating Maestro.

Clean design

A mail app designed from the ground up to have a clean, simple design that’s easy to read and use.

Gestures

Use simple swipes to organize your email, or even add reminders or labels quickly and easily.

Custom Colors

Color-coded accounts so it’s easier to see which emails belong to which linked account.

Favorites

Set up a tab for your favorite people, so you can view emails from your friends and family.

Reminders

Want to see that email six hours from now? No problem. Add a reminder to view it later.

Account Support

Maestro will launch with support for Gmail and Outlook accounts, with more support to come later.

Voice

Maestro works with Cortana, so you can compose and find emails with her help.

Labels

Adding labels works like a charm for an added layer of organization that goes beyond folders.

More Details

How will Maestro make money? Who are our competitors?

Monetization method

Maestro will launch with the first wave of downloads being free. After the promotional period ends, it will cost $2.99 in the app store.

Cost Structure

Costs include ongoing design and development financing, hardware, and marketing investment.

Competitors

There are currently only a few mail apps in the Windows Phone store, such as MetroMail and Mail Plus. However, they don’t offer the robust feature set of Maestro or utilize gestures as succesfully.
